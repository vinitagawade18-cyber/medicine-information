What is Medicine?

Medicine is the science and practice of diagnosing, treating, and preventing diseases. It involves a variety of health care practices to maintain and restore health by the prevention and treatment of illness.

Popular Medicines

Some of the most common medicines include:

  • Paracetamol (for fever and pain)
  • Aspirin (for pain relief and inflammation)
  • Antibiotics (for bacterial infections)
.

Prevention and Health

Prevention is key to maintaining health. Some simple steps include:

  • Washing hands regularly
  • Getting vaccinated
  • Maintaining a healthy diet
